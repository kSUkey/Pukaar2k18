About Us

Welcome To GREEN COMMITTEE - WE CARE THE GREEN MOVEMENT

We Care - The green movement of the National institute of Technology, Raipur was formed with the ultimate aim of educating and creating awareness among the student population about environmental deterioration and the impending perils associated with it and empowering our students to contribute towards safeguarding our environment and reducing our carbon footprint through awareness campaigns and technological interventions.

Go Green

The uniqueness of the movement is its techno-managerial approach to various local, regional, and global environmental issues. The main intention of the committee is to provoke the people from the state of being unconscious about the present environmental issues that we face so that we can do at our personal level.
